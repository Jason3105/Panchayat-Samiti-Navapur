About Navapur Panchayat Samiti

Established in 1962, the Navapur Panchayat Samiti is dedicated to fostering rural development and improving the quality of life for all residents in Navapur Block.

Our mission is to implement government schemes effectively, provide essential services, and create sustainable development opportunities through participatory governance and community engagement.

Over the decades, we have successfully implemented numerous development projects, empowered marginalized communities, and created a model for effective local governance.
