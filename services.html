Rural Housing

Providing affordable housing solutions for rural families

Our housing service aims to provide safe and affordable housing to rural families through various government schemes. We facilitate the construction of new houses, renovation of existing structures, and provide financial assistance to eligible beneficiaries.

Process:

1

Submit application at the Panchayat office with required documents

2

Field verification by Panchayat officials

3

Approval of application by Gram Sabha

4

Issuance of sanction letter

5

Release of funds in installments based on construction progress

Required Documents:

  • Aadhaar Card
  • Income Certificate
  • BPL Card (if applicable)
  • Land ownership documents
  • Bank account details
  • Passport size photographs

Contact Person:

Mr. Ramesh Jadhav, Housing Department

Email: housing@navapurpanchayat.gov.in

Water Supply

Ensuring clean and adequate water supply to all households

The Panchayat Samiti is committed to providing clean and safe drinking water to all rural households through proper infrastructure development. We manage water supply systems, install hand pumps, and implement water conservation measures to ensure sustainable water access.

Process:

1

Submit application for water connection/hand pump installation

2

Technical survey by water department

3

Approval by the Water Committee

4

Payment of connection fee (if applicable)

5

Installation of connection/hand pump

Required Documents:

  • Aadhaar Card
  • Property ownership document
  • Passport size photograph
  • Previous water bill (if any)

Contact Person:

Mrs. Lata Patel, Water Department

Phone: +91 9876543212

Email: water@navapurpanchayat.gov.in

Agriculture

Supporting farmers with modern techniques and resources

Our agriculture services aim to enhance farm productivity and farmer income through technical support, input subsidies, and market linkages. We organize training programs, provide soil testing facilities, and promote sustainable farming practices.

Process:

1

Register with Agriculture Department at Panchayat office

2

Submit application for specific service/subsidy

3

Field verification by agriculture officer

4

Approval and issuance of service/subsidy

Required Documents:

  • Aadhaar Card
  • Land record (7/12 extract)
  • Farmer ID card
  • Bank account details
  • Previous season's crop details

Contact Person:

Mr. Sunil Patil, Agriculture Department

Phone: +91 9876543213

Email: agriculture@navapurpanchayat.gov.in

Healthcare

Promoting public health through primary healthcare facilities

We are committed to improving the health status of rural communities through primary health centers, awareness campaigns, and preventive healthcare measures. Our services include regular health check-ups, immunization, maternal and child healthcare, and health education.

Process:

1

Visit the nearest Primary Health Center

2

Register with your Aadhaar card and contact details

3

Consultation with the medical staff

4

Receive treatment/referral to higher center if needed

Required Documents:

  • Aadhaar Card
  • Previous medical records (if any)
  • Health insurance card (if any)

Contact Person:

Dr. Meena Singh, Health Department

Phone: +91 9876543214

Email: health@navapurpanchayat.gov.in

Education

Enhancing educational infrastructure and quality

Our education services focus on improving school infrastructure, enhancing teaching quality, and ensuring access to education for all rural children. We implement various educational schemes, provide scholarships, and organize educational activities to promote learning.

Electrification

Working towards 100% rural electrification

Our electrification services aim to ensure reliable power supply to all rural households and public spaces. We coordinate with electricity departments for new connections, maintenance of existing infrastructure, and promotion of renewable energy sources.

Process:

1

Submit application for new electricity connection

2

Technical feasibility assessment by electricity department

3

Payment of connection fee (if applicable)

4

Installation of meter and connection establishment

Required Documents:

  • Aadhaar Card
  • Property ownership document
  • Passport size photograph
  • NOC from landlord (for tenants)
